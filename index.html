Enter a number in decimal or integer format and some units (grams, Molals, Liters) then press Molarize

The Elm widget will convert the given value into its equivalent number of mols.

If you enter a unit in grams, it'll ask for the type of molecule so that it can calculate the molecular weight.

If you enter a unit in Molals or Liters, it'll ask for the other amount of the other unit necessary to complete the calculation.

(Example: Try "1" "kg" "Au" for 1 kg of gold. The script will convert to mols of gold)
